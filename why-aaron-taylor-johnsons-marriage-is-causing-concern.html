Why Aaron Taylor-Johnson's Marriage Is Causing Fans To Be Concerned For The Actor

August 2024 Â· 5 minute read

Artist and photographer Sam Taylor-Wood married art dealer Jay Jopling in 1997. The couple were married for 11 years and had two daughters together. One of whom, Angelica, appeared in her mother's directorial film debut Nowhere Boy.

The film starred Aaron Johnson, a young actor who is six years older than Angelica. Sam would begin a relationship with Aaron shortly after splitting from Jopling. They would soon have two children and get married, changing their surnames to Taylor-Johnson.

There are several factors within Sam and Aaron's relationship that seem cause for concern. These questionable factors have led fans to be concerned for Aaron and suspicious of Sam.

Aaron And Sam Insist They Kept It Professional On Set, But A Lot Of People Don't Buy It

Aaron first met Sam at her house when he auditioned for the role of a young John Lennon in Nowhere Boy. Many see this as the first red flag, since a lot of stories within the #MeToo movement begin with an audition at the director's home or hotel room.

However, the official reason Aaron auditioned at Sam's house is that his shooting schedule for the film Kick-Ass didn't allow him to audition with the other actors.

There was an attraction between the two, despite the fact that Sam was 42 while Aaron was still a teenager. Aaron's age at the time of their meeting is still unclear. Some publications reported he was 18, some say he was 19 and other rumors claim he was actually 17.

There were also rumors that Sam was a family friend of Aaron's and knew him as a child. However, these rumors are said to have originated on Twitter and have not been proven.

According to Sam, there was an "intense connection" between the two of them. However, she insisted there was "no funny business at all" while on set.

"We were very professional through the entire film. ... But everyone on set knew. And as soon as we finished, he told me he was going to marry me. We had never been on a date, or even kissed," Sam told Harper's Bazaar in 2019.

However, many don't believe Sam's account. In fact, after Simon Hattenstone of The Guardian visited the set, he wrote the following:

"Whenever the rain comes, she runs inside the house, arm in arm with 19-year-old Aaron Johnson, who plays Lennon. They dance and laugh like teenagers in love. I've never been on a film set with such a strange atmosphere. While the rest of the cast and crew are welcoming, Taylor-Wood and Johnson seem oblivious to the world."

Certain Factors Cause Many To Believe Aaron Was 'Groomed' By His Current Wife

Many people take issue with Aaron and Sam's alleged romantic relationship while filming Nowhere Boy. The reason for this is that Sam was still Aaron's boss during this time. Aaron was also very young and just starting to break out in movies. He was dependent on her to get the part and she had the connections to potentially blacklist him. There was a clear power imbalance in this dynamic.

Aaron said that he proposed to Sam "a year to the minute after we met, exactly one year to the minute."

He told The Telegraph UK, "I knew I wanted a family with her, I knew I wanted kids, and a month later she was pregnant with our first child."

YouTuber Gustaf Genz made a video on the couple's relationship where he said it was natural for Aaron to be "impulsive" at his age, but Sam "should've known better." He also said that when the couple is interviewed together, Aaron always seems to look at Sam for approval when speaking.

The couple would have two children together before marrying in 2012. Aaron later developed a reputation as a stay-at-home dad. Sam would tell The Guardian, "The great thing about Aaron is that he's happy not working and being at home with the kids while I work."
