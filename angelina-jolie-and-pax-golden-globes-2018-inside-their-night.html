Angelina Jolie and Pax Golden Globes 2018: Inside Their Night

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Pax Jolie-Pitt had some cool stories to share with his five siblings this morning. The 14-year-old hit the Golden Globes with mom Angelina Jolie in L.A. on Sunday, January 7.

The pair arrived shortly after the red carpet opened on Sunday, January 7, in matching black ensembles (she wore custom-made Atelier Versace, he chose a velvet tuxedo.) At one point Jolie, 42, stopped to fix Pax’s jacket. Once inside, the By the Sea director and her second-oldest child headed to their table, where they were greeted with name cards. His read Pax Jolie-Pitt, while hers said Angelina Jolie. (Jolie and Brad Pitt split in September 2016 after two years of marriage and 12 years together.) 

During the show, Jolie, who presented Frances McDormand with the Golden Globe for best actress, chatted with seat mate Chris Hemsworth and took several laps around the room to chat with fellow attendees, an onlooker tells Us Weekly.

“Pax seemed very protective over his mom,” the onlooker told Us.
