Jacob Batalon has been surrounded by cancer rumors as the fans realized he had gone bald. Are the rumors true? Learn all about it and more about his weight loss.

Jacob Batalon is a well-known American actor best recognized for his portrayal as Ned Leeds in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) films.

Batalon was born to Filipino parents in Honolulu, Hawaii.

Batalon has seven half-siblings, including a brother and sister from his mother and three brothers and two sisters from his father.

Batalon attended Damien Memorial School after graduating from the private Catholic school St. Anthony’s School and then Kapi’olani Community College to study music theory before dropping out.

He then enrolled in a two-year acting school at the New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts.

Jacob Batalon Weight Loss Explained

Jacob Batalon looks like he has undergone a weight loss transformation.

The Spider-Man actor, who plays Peter Parker’s closest friend Ned Leeds, recently showed off his significant weight reduction on Instagram in preparation for his forthcoming part in the unnamed third Spider-Man film, which is slated to be released in a few days.

Batalon had uploaded a full-body mirror picture on Instagram two months prior, in which he addressed his new look.

The actor flaunts his lean figure in an unbuttoned brown jacket and dark pants in the photo. The actor is accessorized with a pale blue beanie and gold chain necklaces.

He stated that now that he has demonstrated his new appearance, people would cease urging him to lose weight.
