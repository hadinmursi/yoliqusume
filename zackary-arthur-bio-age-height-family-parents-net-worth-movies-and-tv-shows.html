Zackary Arthur Biography

Zackary Arthur is a well-reputed  American child actor best known for his character as “Sammy Sullivan” in “The 5th Wave”. He then appeared as”Zack Novak” in the series “Transparent”. Zach currently has a recurring role as a young Jeff Piccirillo on the Showtime series Kidding.

Zackary Arthur Parents and Family

His mother’s name is Marci Richmond and father’s name is unknown. But his father’s profession was a firefighter. He was raised up by his parents in a friendly and entertaining environment. He has a brother named Aiden Stephens. Arthur was highly encouraged by his mother, Marci Richmond, to secure his career in the acting field and he was also highly interested and dedicated.

Zackary’s brother Aiden is also an actor. Aiden Arthur has worked on several projects with Zackary, including most prominently, Pals (2016), where they are billed first and second and have also worked together in various short films and commercials. Zackary and brother Aiden are also best friends in real life and enjoy playing all sorts of sports, and also playing with their three dogs.

Zachary Arthur Career

2014 was the breakthrough of Zach’s career, He played Zack Novak in the Amazon Studios’ comedy-drama series Transparent along with Jeffrey Tambor, who won the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor Television Series Musical or Comedy. He played Sammy Sullivan, in the film The 5th Wave, along with Chloë Grace Moretz and Nick Robinson, and directed by J Blakeson.

He was one of the hundreds of young actors who auditioned for the role. The film was released on January 22, 2016, by Columbia Pictures. Arthur also has a role in the CBS’ comedy pilot The Half of It. Arthur also appeared in the 20th episode of Grey’s Anatomy season 12, “Trigger Happy”, as a small boy possibly at fault for the paralysis of his friend. He portrayed Josh Ryan in the horror-comedy film Mom and Dad.

He loves reading books, roller coaster lovers, horseback riding and hiking. The youngest of three children, Arthur lives in Southern California with his family including his singer mom, firefighter dad, and twelve-year-old brother Aiden Arthur, who also acts. His sister works in advertising and production. Zack dreams of becoming a filmmaker making films that inspire people to make a difference in the world.

Acting since the age of six, Zackary has learned to master various accents for various projects. Arthur has dozens of short films and commercials to his credit, including spots for Honda, Best Buy, and voice-over for Build-A-Bear and continues to study his craft at Gray Acting Studios. Zackary’s other talents include; all sports, singing, break dancing, climbing trees, and drawing.
