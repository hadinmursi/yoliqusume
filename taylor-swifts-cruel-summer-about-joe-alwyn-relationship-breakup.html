Taylor Swifts 'Cruel Summer' Reveals The Tragic Truth About Her Joe Alwyn Relationship, According

September 2024 · 6 minute read

Highlights

  • "Cruel Summer" is a standout song for Taylor Swift fans, thanks to its catchy beat and intense lyrics.
  • One fan's interpretation suggests that the song could be about the start of Swift's relationship with Joe Alwyn, hinting at underlying problems.
  • Swift's lyrics in "Cruel Summer" possibly allude to the challenges of dating someone famous and the need for secrecy.

While Taylor Swift's fans generally love most of her music, there's something special about "Cruel Summer." It not only has a great, catchy beat, but the lyrics are compelling and intense. Ever since Swift's album "Lover" was released in 2019, fans were drawn to this particular tune.

Although fans always try to guess what the beloved star is singing about, one fan had an especially fascinating interpretation of Taylor Swift's "Cruel Summer." According to this fan, the beautifully written lyrics could be referring to the start of her romance with Joe Alwyn. From this interpretation, it's possible to say that the alleged problems between them were there all along.

Taylor Swift's 'Cruel Summer' Could Be About Her Dramatic Relationship With Joe Alwyn

Taylor Swift's "Cruel Summer" could be interpreted as a summer fling that is as fun as it is intense and painful. If fans view it through the lens of the singer's breakup with Joe Alwyn, they can assume that perhaps it tells the story of why they ended their six-year romance.

Redditor @kalimdore posted in the Taylor Swift SubReddit and shared their theory about "Cruel Summer." They wrote:

"It’s about summer (mostly late summer) 2016 when she was secretly seeing Joe. They were casually hooking up no strings attached because it “wasn’t a good time” for her rep lol and both of them didn’t want the media to get wind of anything."

The fan continued, "So they were not in a relationship, he was likely hooking up with other girls in between and she was trying to pretend she felt the same chill way about it." According to this fan, Swift developed strong and real feelings for Alwyn quickly, but the romance wasn't official yet. But since she liked him so much, this meant that the situation could end in heartbreak, which it definitely did when Alwyn and Swift broke up in 2023.

The fan continued, "She knew it was dangerous to get close to someone she liked so much. Playing a game that would hurt her if she fell for him whilst pretending she was just in it for the fun. Devils roll the dice, basically taking a dangerous but exciting gamble on getting close to him, angels roll their eyes because they know they’ve lost her to this."

What Is The Meaning Of Taylor Swift's 'Cruel Summer'?

Taylor Swift doesn't appear to have shared what her hit song "Cruel Summer" means, which of course leaves it up to interpretation by fans. However, it does seem likely that she was writing about Joe Alwyn since she has talked about how another song on the album, "Lover" refers to a special moment in a relationship.

According to Capital FM, Swift talked about "Lover" at the Nashville Songwriter Awards in 2020 and said, “I think that’s a really beautiful thing when people choose to spend their life together. And so it’s sort of a celebration of taking that very adult step of living with someone and deciding this is the path forward for you, walking alongside somebody else." Since she was dating Alwyn at the time, it's clear that she wrote "Lover" about him, and she must have written other songs on the album about him as well.
