Kylie Rae: Nobody Made Me Leave AEW. Im Grateful For The Opportunity

August 2024 · 1 minute read

Former AEW superstar Kylie Rae was released from the promotion a few months ago shortly after being advertised for a match with Leva Bates at Fyter Fest.

Speculation and rumors swirled about whether she was forced to leave the promotion or if she genuinely wanted out. Kylie took to her Instagram today to set the record straight.

Kylie firmly stated that nobody made her leave AEW and she was grateful for the opportunities she received. She said, “I’ve always tried to be a good person but I make many mistakes along the way. I needed to get my mind, body, and soul right with God. I wish I had the answers but the truth I don’t. We all go through obstacles in life and we may want all the answers but we don’t need them.”
