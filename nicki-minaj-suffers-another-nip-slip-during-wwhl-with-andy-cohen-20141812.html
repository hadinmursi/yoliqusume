Nicki Minaj Suffers Another Nip Slip During WWHL with Andy Cohen

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Can Nicki Minaj keep her clothes on?! The “Anaconda” singer suffered yet another nip slip on Wednesday, Dec. 17, when she appeared on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen.

Within the first 20 seconds of her appearance, Minaj was broadcast with more than just cleavage showing from her tight, incredibly low-cut black bustier.

Apparently oblivious to the situation, Cohen and Minaj conducted their introductions, but fans instantly took to Twitter to marvel at the wardrobe malfunction and post pictures of the curvy singer’s appearance, complete with areola peeking out of her left bra cup.

“Trying to be classy, and you had a nip slip @NICKIMINAJ @Andy #wwhl @Bravotv @BravoWWHL,” wrote one fan, complete with a screen grab of the incident.

But in an ironic turn of events, talk then moved to Minaj’s previous wardrobe malfunctions (one of her prior nip slips on Good Morning America, and her completely unzipped dress at this year’s MTV Video Music Awards) with no mention of her very recent mishap — something Twitter appreciated!

“@NICKIMINAJ is talking about a nip slip while CURRENTLY having a nip slip. @BravoWWHL, help her!,” another viewer wrote via Twitter.

“Which was worse?” Minaj was asked by a caller, about the GMA and VMA incidents.

“Definitely the nip slip,” Minaj revealed. “Because the dress malfunction didn’t show anything.”
