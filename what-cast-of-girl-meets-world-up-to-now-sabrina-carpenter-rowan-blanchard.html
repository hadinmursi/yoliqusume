What The Cast Of Girl Meets World Is Up To Now

September 2024 Â· 5 minute read

Girl Meets World ran for three seasons on the Disney Channel from 2014 to 2017. The sequel to ABC's Boy Meets World, it followed the lives of Riley Matthews, her family, and her best friends. The cast was comprised of a bunch of young actors who had yet to make a name for themselves in the industry. Following the debut of the show, many of the young cast members shot to stardom and have received many opportunities over the years. The show also starred a couple of the original actors from Boy Meets World, Danielle Fishel as Topanga Lawrence and Ben Savage as Cory Matthews.

Related: 10 Things Rowan Blanchard Has Done Since 'Girl Meets World'

It has been a few years now since the show aired it's final episode, so fans may be curious as to what the cast is doing now. From singing careers to Netflix movies to having babies, here's what the cast of Girl Meets World has been up to.

8 Danielle Fishel Is A Mom

Danielle Fishel is now a mom to two little boys, Adler, and Keaton. She is happily married to her husband, Jensen Karp, and resides in Orange County with her family. She has also spent her post-Girl Meets World life directing episodes of multiple Disney Channel series such as Raven's Home and Sydney to the Max.

7 Sabrina Carpenter Is A Successful Music Artist And Actress

Sabrina Carpenter has made a huge name for herself in Hollywood as both an actress and a music artist. She has multiple successful studio albums under her belt and has starred in several popular movies available on streaming services such as Tall Girl on Netflix and Clouds on Disney+. She can also add Broadway actress to her resume as she starred in a few performances of the Mean Girls musical just before the world shut down due to the pandemic. She is a super talented triple threat, as she's able to sing, dance and act. The girl has a bright future ahead of her and she's only getting started.

6 Rowan Blanchard Is An Activist

The star of Girl Meets World, Rowan Blanchard, went on to play a small role in Disney's film adaptation of A Wrinkle in Time. She also guest-starred on several episodes of The Goldbergs on ABC and plays Alexandra Cavill on TNT's series Snowpiercer. Blanchard has also used her platform to fight for things she believes in such as the Black Lives Matter movement, which she participated in during the summer of 2020. She has also spoken up about her sexuality, coming out as queer at a young age.

5 Peyton Meyer Was In 'He's All That'

Peyton Meyer, the love interest from Girl Meets World whom a lot of fans swooned over, recently had a role as the villain and ex-boyfriend of Addison Rae's character in Netflix's He's All That. He also had a recurring role on the ABC sitcom, American Housewife, playing the role of Trip Windsor. Meyer has also spent the past few years traveling when he can, as it's something he loves to do. He's spent time in Amsterdam as well as Turks and Caicos.

4 August Maturo Has Been Making Movies

The youngest member of the Girl Meets World family, August Maturo, who played Riley's brother, Auggie, was in the movie, The Nun, as well as the film, Shepard: The Story of a Jewish Dog. He also starred in the movie Slapface and appeared in an episode of The Conners on ABC. The actor is also currently attending high school.

3 Ben Savage Has Been Guest Starring On Shows

The actor behind Cory Matthews has been spending his post-Girl Meets World years guest-starring on various television shows, such as Homeland, Criminal Minds, Speechless, Still The King, and Leftovers. He also filmed a commercial for Panera Bread in early 2021 with his former co-star, Fishel, that made many fans giddy with joy. Savage keeps a pretty low profile in Hollywood and on social media, so not much is known about his personal life, but he definitely enjoys being an uncle to his brother's kids.

2 Corey Fogelmanis Has Been Making Movies

Alongside his photography hobby which can be seen on his Instagram account, the actor behind the iconic role of Farkle has been working on several projects over the last few years including the Netflix movie Moxie and the horror film, Ma, alongside Octavia Spencer. He has also spent time supporting his former co-star and best friend, Carpenter in her many professional endeavors. He enjoys promoting her music on his Instagram by singing his own versions of her songs. It's quite hilarious.

1 Amir Mitchell-Townes Is A Rapper

The actor who played Zay Babineaux is actually the son of DJ Jazzy Jeff and is a rapper outside of his acting career. He goes by the stage name Uhmeer and has a handful of singles out as well as an EP. He enjoys making music with his dad and continues to act when he's able. He had a role in an episode of the Starz series Power Book II: Ghost that aired at the end of 2020, but other than that, Mitchell-Townes is mostly focusing on his music career.
