Rey Mysterio Would Love To Face Dominik Mysterio In A Hair vs. Mask Match

August 2024 · 1 minute read

Rey Mysterio says he would love to have a hair versus mask match against Dominik Mysterio.

In April, Rey Mysterio stated that he would pitch a hair versus mask match against his son, Dominik. The reigning WWE United States Champion feels the same way a few months later.

Speaking with Ariel Helwani on The MMA Hour, Rey Mysterio was asked whether there was more to his storyline with Dominik. (H/t Jeremy Lambert of Fightful for the transcription).

“I hope not. I think there’s still more to give, for both of us,” he said.

Helwani then brought up a potential hair vs. mask match between Rey ans his son. The veteran responded by saying, “Why not? I would love that. I don’t think we’ve ever seen that here. Imagine him bald. Don’t imagine me without the mask, you already know.”

During the interview, Rey stated that he will have a face-to-face with Logan Paul on the October 20 episode of WWE SmackDown. More information is available here.
