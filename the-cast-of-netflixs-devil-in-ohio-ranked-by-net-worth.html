The Cast Of Netflix's Devil In Ohio, Ranked By Net Worth

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Netflix’s suspense thriller series Devil in Ohio has been streaming for nearly a fortnight now. The Daria Polatin mini-series became available on the platform on September 2 and spent the following week in the number one spot on the list of Top 10 TV Shows in the U.S., before it was eventually dethroned by the returning Cobra Kai for Season 5.

According to Rotten Tomatoes, the show is about “a psychiatrist [who] takes in a young patient who escaped from a cult, putting her family and life in danger.” The two leading roles are played by Bones star Emily Deschanel and Canadian actress Madeleine Arthur as Dr. Suzanne Mathis and Mae respectively.

Parenthood actor Sam Jaeger features as Dr. Mathis’ real estate developer husband, Peter, while Gerardo Celasco from How To Get Away with Murder portrays Detective Lopez.

Alisha Newton (Heartland, Percy Jackson: Sea of Monsters) and DC’s Bradley Stryker also make up part of the cast line-up for the show. But who among them has managed to amass the most wealth?

9 Alisha Newton Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Canadian star Alisha Newton is known for playing the character Georgina “Georgie” Fleming Morris in the CBC drama series Heartland. She has also starred in movies like Scorched Earth and Percy Jackson: Sea of Monsters. In Devil in Ohio, she portrays Helen Mathis, daughter of Dr. Suzanne and Peter Mathis.

Newton is believed to be currently worth around $1 million.

8 Tahmoh Penikett Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Like Alisha Newton, Tahmoh Penikett is also estimated to have a net worth of around $1 million. Also born in Canada, the actor is otherwise best known for his role as Karl “Helo” Agathon in Syfy’s Battlestar Galactica.

Penikett features in Devil in Ohio as a character simply known as Malachi.

7 Samantha Ferris Has A Net Worth Of $1.5 Million

Samantha Ferris is not one of the main cast members in Devil in Ohio, as she appears in a limited role over the course of only four out of eight episodes. She is definitely in the conversation for one of the richest, though, with an approximated net worth of $1.5 million.

Ferris’ filmography includes other titles such as Supernatural and The 4400.

6 Gerardo Celasco Has A Net Worth Of $1.5 Million

Gerardo Celasco is perhaps best known among fans of daytime soap operas, with roles in NBC’s Passions and Tyler Perry’s The Haves and the Have Nots for the Oprah Winfrey Network (OWN).

He plays Detective Lopez in Devil in Ohio, tasked with investigating Mae’s case. With a net worth of around $1.5 million, Celasco is also said to be as rich as Samantha Ferris.

5 Madeleine Arthur Has A Net Worth Of $2 Million

Madeleine Arthur is only 25 years old, but she is already building up an impressive acting career. As well as her main role in Devil in Ohio, she is also set to feature in an equally core character in Blockbuster, an upcoming Netflix workplace comedy.

At the moment, Arthur is believed to boast a net worth of $2 million, a figure that is only likely to keep going up.

4 Bradley Stryker Has A Net Worth Of $2 Million

Bradley Stryker has been in the business for a much longer period of time, having started his acting career in 2001. He played Trey Atwood in Fox’s teen drama series The O.C., and is also remembered for his portrayal of DC Comics character Deadshot in Smallville.

Despite this great wealth of experience, Stryker has not managed to amass more wealth beyond his current, estimated $2 million.

3 Sam Jaeger Has A Net Worth Of $2 Million

Like Bradley Stryker, Sam Jaeger has been around the block for quite a while. Yet also like Stryker, his net worth stands at around the $2 million mark. Jaeger started acting in 1999, and is recognizable for his work in NBC’s Parenthood, as well as movies like Traffic and Behind Enemy Lines.

In more recent years, he has featured in the TV shows Tell Me a Story and Why Women Kill. His character in Devil in Ohio is called Sheriff Wilkins.

2 Marci T. House Has A Net Worth Of $5 Million

Day of the Dead actress Marci T. House only plays a recurring role in Devil in Ohio, as a character called Adele Thornton. When it comes to rankings of wealth, however, only one colleague on the show can eclipse her reported $5 million net worth.

House has also appeared in other television shows like Midnight Mass, Tribal and the Charmed reboot.

1 Emily Deschanel Has A Net Worth Of $30 Million

Emily Deschanel is so far ahead of the pack in terms of the richest Devil in Ohio cast member, that her net worth is practically double that of everyone else on this list combined.

Emily has a younger sister named Zooey, who is just about as successful as she is. The older sibling is richer by about $5 million. She would have made a good chunk of her $30 million net worth from her role as Temperance Brennan in the crime procedural series Bones on Fox between 2005 and 2017.
