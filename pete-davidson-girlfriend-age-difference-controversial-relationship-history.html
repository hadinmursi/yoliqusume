The Age Difference Between Pete Davidson And His Girlfriends Hasn't Always Been Controversial

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Pete Davidson has gained a reputation for his highly publicized relationships with several famous women, particularly those who are a lot older than he is. He first gained fame as a cast member on the popular TV show Saturday Night Live and has since become known for his humor and often controversial personal life.

His dating history has been a widely reported topic of interest in the media and popular culture, largely because he has been romantically linked to a number of high-profile women including household names such as Kim Kardashian, Ariana Grande, and Emily Ratajkowski. Many of these relationships have been short-lived, but they have nonetheless contributed to his notoriety as a Hollywood ladies' man.

Pete Davidson has become known for dating older women, he had a 13-year age difference with Kim Kardashian and a 20-year age gap with actress Kate Beckinsale, however, he hasn't always been involved in relationships with a controversial age difference. Here are some of his more age appropriate relationships...

8 Inside Pete Davidson's Relationship With Fellow Comedian Carly Aquilino

Pete Davidson briefly dated comedian Carly Aquilino in 2015, the pair had an age difference of 3 years. At the time, Pete was a new cast member on Saturday Night Live and Carly was a comedian and actress known for her appearances on MTV's Girl Code.

While things never got serious between the pair, they seemed to part on good terms. According to Us Weekly, the comedian commented on her ex's engagement to pop star Ariana Grande years later in 2018. Aquilino shared her thoughts on their romance in a post to Instagram, writing: “I know I’m the 9 billionth person to text you today about this, but…I can’t."

7 Pete Davidson And Cazzie David Were A Serious Couple For 2 Years

Pete Davidson dated The Umbrella Academy actress and daughter of comedian Larry David from 2016 to 2018. The pair shared a slight age difference of one year.

They were fairly private during their relationship, but occasionally made public appearances together. Pete Davidson confirmed that they broke up in May 2018 during a candid interview on Complex’s Open Late with Peter Rosenberg, saying: "We’re not together anymore. Very talented girl, she’ll be great and she’ll be fine. Yeah, I think she’ll be OK."

In her 2020 book of personal essays, titled No One Asked for This, Cazzie opened up about her breakup with Pete Davidson and how it negatively affected her mental health to see him move on so quickly and publicly with superstar Ariana Grande.

6 Pete Davidson's High-Profile Relationship With Ariana Grande Took The World By Storm

Pete Davidson's relationship with singer Ariana Grande began in May 2018, the couple even announced a surprise engagement, but their relationship only lasted for a total of 5 months. The pair shared a minuscule 5-month age difference, with them both being born in 1993. The couple's whirlwind romance was highly publicized and drew a lot of attention from fans and the media.

Related: How Many Tattoos Did Pete Davidson Get For Kim Kardashian And Ariana Grande?

Pete and Ariana initially met in 2016 when she made a guest appearance on Saturday Night Live, where he was a cast member. They began dating after they both ended long-term relationships in 2018 - Pete with Cazzie David, and Ariana with late rapper Mac Miller. Their relationship progressed quickly, and they were engaged just a few weeks after making their relationship public.

The couple often shared affectionate messages and photos on social media, with Ariana even writing a song about their relationship titled "Pete Davidson." Despite the public's fascination with their relationship, Pete and Ariana's engagement was short-lived.

In October 2018, the couple announced that they had broken up and called off their engagement. Ariana later famously referenced Pete in her hit single 'thank u, next', singing: "Even almost got married. And for Pete, I'm so thankful."

5 inside Pete Davidson's Relationship With Margaret Qualley

Maid Actress Margaret Qualley and Pete Davidson had a brief summer fling, they began dating in August 2019 and broke up months later in October. The pair had a slight one-year age difference, and were seen together at several public events. They both attended Venice Film Festival and the premiere of Margaret's movie Seberg, where they were pictured together. The couple also made headlines for their public displays of affection, such as when they were photographed holding hands while taking a stroll in Italy.

Actress Andie MacDowell, who is Qualley’s mother commented on their relationship, saying: "She has a beautiful relationship with him, but I don’t want to step on her relationship too much, they have a nice relationship."

4 Pete Davidson Ended His Fling With Kaia Gerber As A Result Of Mental Health Struggles

While Pete Davidson is often in controversy for dating older women, his relationship with model Kaia Gerber in October 2019 was controversial because he was considered far too old to be dating her. At the time of their relationship, Pete was 26 years old, while Kaia was only 18.

This age difference raised eyebrows and concerns among some fans and media outlets, who felt that the relationship was inappropriate due to the power dynamic and maturity levels involved. According to Pagesix, Kaia's famous mother and supermodel Cindy Crawford hoped that their relationship would "fizzle out".

The relationship did in fact fizzle out, with the two reportedly breaking up a few months later in January of the next year. Pete Davidson confirmed his split from Kaia Gerber, and blamed their breakup on his personal struggles and their significant age difference, saying: "She’s very young, and I’m f–king going through a lot, and it was before I went to rehab. It just wasn’t the right place or the right time at all.”

3 How Pete Davidson And Phoebe Dynevor's Long-Distance Relationship Was Cut Short

Rumors of a relationship between Pete Davidson and Bridgerton star Phoebe Dynevor first arose in March 2021, the pair had a two-year age difference and were spotted holding hands in the Staffordshire, UK.

The couple then made their first public outing at the tennis tournament Wimbledon in July of the same year, where they were photographed kissing each other. However, their romance was short-lived, with the pair breaking up in August of the same year after just five months together. The reason for their breakup was cited to be because of distance, with Davidson living in New York while Dynevor lives in England.

2 Pete Davidson Briefly Dated Emily Ratajkowski After His Controversial Relationship With Kim Kardashian

Following his highly publicized breakup from reality star Kim Kardashian in August 2022, Davidson reportedly began dating model and social media personality Emily Ratajkowski in November 2022.

The pair were set up by mutual friends, and had a two-year age difference, making their public debut as a couple when they were pictured holding hands. However, their relationship was short-lived, with the couple breaking up the following month in December 2022.

1 Pete Davidson Is Currently Dating Co-Star Chase Sui Wonders

Pete Davidson was first rumored to be dating his Bodies Bodies Bodies co-star 26-year-old actor Chase Sui Wonders in December, just days after his breakup with Emily Ratajkowski. The pair have a 3-year age difference and were spotted together at a New York Rangers game alongside their co-star Rachel Sennott. The couple were later seen cuddling in a Brooklyn restaurant in January 2023.
