Which Dancing with the Star celebrity was the most ridiculous inclusion?

During the 29th season of Dancing with the Stars, Carole Baskin from The Tiger King was partnered with Pasha Pashkov. While there have been several controversial celebrities who’ve performed on Dancing with the Stars, there are two reasons why Baskin’s inclusion was the most ridiculous. First off, The Tiger King portrayed Baskin as a villain so her inclusion was going to turn off some people. More importantly, even though The Tiger King was a sensation after it was released, people moved on from the show surprisingly quickly. As a result, most people didn’t really care about Baskin by the time she appeared on Dancing with the Stars.

What makes Big Brother feel like a cheap show?

Over the years, the production of Big Brother has been improved in many ways. The most obvious example of that is the fact that the cameras are of much better quality now than when the show began. Despite that, there is one element of Big Brother that makes the show feel cheap far too often. During far too many of Big Brother’s challenges, the decorations that are used look like they will pulled together in a couple of hours. Considering that it is easier than ever for people to find and buy high-quality decorations, there is no excuse for a major network show to look so cheap.
