Sophie Turner Bonds With Joe Jonas Ex Taylor Swift Over Drinks As He Keeps The Kids Amid Divorce

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Sophie Turner and Taylor Swift were seen together in New York, sparking speculation that they are bonding over their shared history with Joe Jonas.
  • Sophie and Taylor first met in 2019 and have remained friends since then, with Sophie describing Taylor as a "genuinely kind person."
  • Sophie and Joe's divorce is ongoing, and there have been rumors of struggles in their relationship, but they have not publicly discussed the details.

Sophie Turner made a bold statement by hanging out with Joe Jonas’s ex-girlfriend Taylor Swift in the midst of their ongoing divorce.

The former Game of Thrones star, 27, was captured arm-in-arm with Taylor, 33, on Tuesday as they shared an evening in New York. Following a meal at a restaurant, the duo made their way to Temple Bar, situated on Lafayette Street.

For their girls' night out, Sophie sported a gray crop top paired with loose-fitting, coordinated pants. She accessorized with a black purse while draping a black sweater around her waist.

For her part, Taylor was seen in a red mini-dress, complemented by an oversized denim jacket. She finished her look with brown knee-high boots, and a petite green handbag.

Joe Jonas Broke Up With Taylor Swift In A Phone Call

Sophie and Taylor’s evening out speaks volumes, considering the singer’s history with Sophie’s estranged husband, Joe Jonas.

The two women have rarely been seen hanging out prior to Tuesday even, with many fans speculating Sophie and Taylor are bonding over the fact that Joe is now an ex to both of them. They apparently first met in 2019 during their joint appearance on BBC One's The Graham Norton Show (the same year Sophie and Joe wed).

After this meeting, Sophie described Taylor Swift as a "genuinely kind person" in an interview with Extra, and they have remained friends ever since.

Taylor was briefly involved with the Jonas Brothers singer in July 2008, but their relationship came to an end just a few months later in October of the same year. Rumor has it that Joe Jonas initiated the breakup with a succinct 25-second phone call.

Taylor discussed this on the Ellen DeGeneres Show shortly after, stating, “I'm not even gonna be able to remember the boy who broke up with me over the phone in 25 seconds when I was 18.”

Joe later defended himself against Taylor’s comments, saying he wasn’t the one who chose to end the phone call so abruptly. “'For those who have expressed concern over the ‘27 second’ phone call, I called to discuss feelings with the other person,” he wrote on Myspace. “Those feelings were obviously not well received. I did not end the conversation."

It appears Taylor and Sophie kept in touched after initially meeting, and now that the actress is on the rebound from their mutual ex, it appears Taylor is trying to show her a good time.

Joe Is Taking Care Of The Kids While Sophie Travels

Sophie and Joe’s divorce remains ongoing, but there’s been intense speculation about the situation. The couple’s two young daughters are reportedly in their father’s care, despite him being on tour with the Jonas Brothers, as Sophie was filming a movie in the UK until recently.

There have been allegations the couple struggled following the birth of their second child last year, and also rumors that Sophie regretted getting married and having kids so young.

Despite a joint statement confirming their divorce, Sophie and Joe are remaining relatively silent on the details of their split.
