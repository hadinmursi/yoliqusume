    How do I get a refund from Amazon UK?

    To request a refund:
  • Go to Your Orders.
  • Locate the order.
  • Select Problem with order.
  • Select your problem from the list.
  • Select Request refund.
  • Enter your comments in the text box.
  • Select Submit.

    • How do I get a refund for undelivered items on Amazon?

    To request a refund on an eligible order:
  • Go to Your Orders.
  • Locate your order in the list and click Problem with order.
  • Select “Package didn’t arrive” from the list.
  • Select Request refund.
  • Enter your comments in the text box.
  • Select Submit. Note: Refund requests can take up to one week to investigate.
