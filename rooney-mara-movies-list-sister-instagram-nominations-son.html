April 17, 1985 (age 38) – Patricia Rooney Mara, widely known as Rooney Mara, is an American actress with a successful career spanning television and film. Born into the prestigious Rooney and Mara families, Rooney has made a name for herself with remarkable performances, earning several accolades and nominations, including two Academy Award nominations, a British Academy Film Award nomination, and two Golden Globe nominations.

Mara’s journey in the world of acting began with appearances in television and independent films, with her breakthrough role in the coming-of-age drama Tanner Hall (2009). However, it was her supporting role in David Fincher’s drama film The Social Network (2010) that brought her recognition.

The turning point in her career came when Rooney Mara starred as Lisbeth Salander in Fincher’s thriller The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo (2011). Her portrayal earned her critical acclaim and an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress. From then on, she continued to impress with leading roles in successful movies like Side Effects (2013), Her (2013), and Carol (2015). For her performance in Carol, she won the Best Actress award at the Cannes Film Festival and received an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress.

Rooney Mara’s filmography is filled with diverse and challenging roles. She showcased her talent in Lion (2016), A Ghost Story (2017), and Mary Magdalene (2018). After a short break, she returned to the big screen, delivering exceptional performances in the psychological thriller Nightmare Alley (2021) and the drama Women Talking (2022).

Beyond her acting career, Mara is also known for her philanthropy and advocacy work. She is actively involved in overseeing the Uweza Foundation, which supports empowerment programs for children and families in the Kibera slum of Nairobi. Additionally, she is the founder of the vegan clothing line Hiraeth Collective.

In her personal life, Rooney Mara has been in a relationship with actor Joaquin Phoenix since 2016. The couple got engaged and welcomed their first child, a son named River Lee, in August 2020.

Rooney Mara’s dedication to her craft, along with her philanthropic efforts, has made her a respected and influential figure in the entertainment industry. With a remarkable list of movies, a supportive partner, and a passion for making a positive impact, Rooney Mara’s career continues to flourish, and her future endeavors are highly anticipated by her fans and the film community.
