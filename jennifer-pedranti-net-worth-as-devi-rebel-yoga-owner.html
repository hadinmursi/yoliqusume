Jennifer Runs A Yoga Studio

Jennifer runs a successful yoga studio, Devi Rebel Yoga, in Rancho Santa Margarita, Orange County. She appears to be focused on the world of health, fitness, and yoga.

The small women-owned business is passionate about overall wellness and educating the community offering a wide variety of services to improve the state of mind, body, and spirit.

It intends to create a platform that would embody nurturing, powerful, dynamic, and gentle qualities that would ultimately honor the harmony of nature in one’s yoga practice.

The instructors guide their clients to keep their bodies fit. They believe overall fitness gives the physical strength to tackle every difficulty in life.

A home to DR Wellness, Devi Rebel Yoga offers Yoga Therapy, Reiki Healing, and Holistic Health Coaching.

They use the holistic approach to assist clients in achieving optimal longevity, fitness, and mind-body health.

Pedranti serves as one of the co-owners of Devi Rebel Yoga and promotes her business through inspiring Instagram posts. Her IG posts also suggest that she is also a yoga instructor.

In an IG post, she said her business partners are “INCREDIBLE” at what they do. “If they can help and educate Me.. they can help anyone,” the RHOC new cast continued.

Devi’s teachers and instructors are a group of dedicated multi-talented Yog and Fitness trainers. The CPR Certified trainers are insured and have a variety of Yoga Alliance Certifications.

Employing a variety of talents, the studio is able to offer extended classes from guided meditation to a variety of heart-pounding, sweat-producing classes. They also have private yoga and wellness.

Their memberships are all auto-renewing and can be altered anytime without a contract.

Devi Rebel Yoga has different packages, including 4 (Yogi) Classes costing $80 per month and 8 (Devi) classes costing $120 per month.
