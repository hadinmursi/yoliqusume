Kevin Cox is the brother of the Real Housewives of Potomac star Monique Samuels, who is in the spotlight following her cast in Love & Marriage: DC. Let’s find out more about Kevin’s age and details about his personal life.

Kevin Cox is the younger sibling of Monique Samuels. She and her husband Chris Samuels stars in the new series Love & Marriage: DC which premiered on OWN Network on May 14. The series follows the personal and professional lives of three successful couples living in the Maryland area.

Cox has two sisters, Monique and Tosh Mac. The three are very close to each other. Monique is a tv star and an influencer whereas Tosh is an Artist Manager at StudioNation.

Who Is Monique Samuels Brother Kevin Cox?

Kevin Cox is the brother of the Love & Marriage: DC star Monique Samuels. He runs Monique’s fan page, The Boss Squad on Instagram, showing the support he has for his sibling. He is a jolly fellow who talks in various podcasts about his sister and her show.

Moreover, Kevin is one of the three children born to Jeanette Cox and her husband. The family is very close to each other. In a May 2020 post on Instagram by Monique, she posted a beautiful group picture of Jeanette and her three children wishing her a happy mother’s day.

Currently, Kevin is on a hiatus from Instagram. His last post was on January 10, 2020, where he posted a video of his interview on the Bravo Betch podcast, saying that he needed to ‘detox’ from social media for a few months.
