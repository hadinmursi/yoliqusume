Gylfi Sigurdsson’s arrest has gone viral on Twitter. He is not in jail, despite reports to the contrary.

Gylfi Sigurdsson, also known as Gylfi Thor Sigurdsson, is an Icelandic attacking midfielder who currently plays for Premier League side Everton and the Iceland national team.

Similarly, Gylfi began his professional career in the Championship with Reading and was transferred to 1899 Hoffenheim in 2010, which was Reading’s greatest sell at the time.

Based on the report of Wikipedia, he was named Reading’s Player of the Season in 2009–10 and Hoffenheim’s Player of the Season in 2010–11.

Likewise, he returned to English football with Swansea City for a season before joining Tottenham Hotspur for an estimated £8 million transfer price.
