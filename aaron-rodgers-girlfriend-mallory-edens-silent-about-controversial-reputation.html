According to People, Edens talked about this injury in an Instagram post in October 2023. She wrote about the fear that she felt during this time and said, "I’ve spent a lot of time lately thinking about the kid who woke up in that hospital bed, terrified she would never run a lap around a track or read a book again. How scary it was to lose everything I thought I knew about myself at that age in the blink of an eye."
